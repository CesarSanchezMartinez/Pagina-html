Les Paul 70s Deluxe

The Les Paul™ 70s Deluxe takes you back to a legendary rock moment, with the genuine feel of a rediscovered relic

First introduced in 1969, the Deluxe saw the introduction of the mini humbucker™ to the Les Paul line. Mini humbuckers retain the hum-free performance of their full-size cousins, but with a slightly more transparent and bright tonality. The new Deluxe has features inspired by those of early 1970s models, with an unrelieved mahogany body and bound maple top, a bound mahogany neck with a rounded C profile, retro-style Keystone tuners, GraphTech® nut and a traditional Les Paul control layout with 2 volume and 2 tone controls wired with Orange Drop® capacitors. Available in classic Goldtop and 1970s Cherry Sunburst gloss nitrocellulose lacquer finishes.
